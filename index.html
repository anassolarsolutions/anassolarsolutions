Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about solar and electrical services

What is the cost of a complete solar system for a house in Pakistan?
A complete 5kW solar system for a typical house in Pakistan costs between Rs. 450,000 to Rs. 650,000 depending on the quality of components, installation complexity, and battery backup requirements.
How much can I save on electricity bills with solar panels?
Most households save 70-90% on their electricity bills. A properly sized system can eliminate your electricity bill completely through net metering.
What is the lifespan of solar panels in Pakistan's climate?
Solar panels typically last 25-30 years. They come with 25-year performance warranties, guaranteeing 80-85% efficiency even after 25 years of use.
Which is better: on-grid or off-grid solar system?
On-grid is better if you have reliable grid connection as it allows net metering. Off-grid is necessary in areas with frequent power outages but requires batteries which increase cost.
How long does it take to install a solar system?
A typical residential solar installation takes 3-7 days depending on system size and complexity. Commercial installations may take 2-4 weeks.
Do solar panels work during load shedding?
Only hybrid or off-grid systems with battery backup work during load shedding. Pure on-grid systems shut down during power outages for safety reasons.
What maintenance do solar panels require?
Minimal maintenance - occasional cleaning of panels (every 6 months) and annual professional inspection of electrical components.
Can I get a loan for solar installation in Pakistan?
Yes, several banks including HBL, UBL, and Bank Alfalah offer solar financing with flexible payment plans and competitive interest rates.
What is net metering and how does it work?
Net metering allows you to sell excess solar electricity back to the grid. Your meter runs backward when you produce more than you consume, reducing your bill.
Which solar panels are best for Pakistani weather?
Monocrystalline panels from brands like Jinko, Longi, Canadian Solar, and JA Solar perform best in Pakistan's hot climate with high temperatures.
